# Oneida

<!--Add Short Description and History of Language Here-->

Oneida has three variations/dialects:
1. Oneida (Canada) (Also Known as: Oneida (Southwold))
2. Oneida (Wisconsin) (Also Known as: Oneida (Green Bay))
3. Oneida (New York)
